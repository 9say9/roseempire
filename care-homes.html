Why care homes choose Rose Empire mattress protectors

Care home procurement teams need bedding that survives industrial laundry cycles, protects mattresses from fluids, and meets infection-control expectations without compromising resident comfort. Our wholesale mattress protector range is built for exactly that environment.

  • WQMP waterproof quilted protectors — silent TPU backing, quilted comfort layer, OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified
  • Terry waterproof protectors — hotel-grade cotton terry surface, deep elastic skirt, preferred for high-turnover beds
  • QMP quilted protectors — breathable non-waterproof option for lower-risk rooms or over-mattress layering
  • Goose feather & duck down pillows — standard size wholesale pairs with BS EN 12935 test report available

Compliance documentation for facilities teams

We provide documentation procurement teams expect: UK mattress protector test report (PDF), BS EN 12935 pillow test report, and our wholesale catalog with trade pricing. OEKO-TEX certification supports due diligence for resident-facing textiles.
