Wholesale bedding — frequently asked questions

Common questions from UK hotels, care homes, guest houses and retail buyers about Rose Empire trade supply.

What is the minimum order quantity for Rose Empire wholesale bedding?

Our wholesale MOQ is 20 pieces per product size (one trade box). Volume discounts apply on larger bulk orders for hotels, care homes and retail distributors across the UK.

Does Rose Empire deliver mattress protectors and pillows across the UK?

Yes. We supply wholesale bedding UK-wide including mainland England, Scotland, Wales, Scottish Highlands and Northern Ireland. Freight is quoted by destination and order size at checkout.

What wholesale mattress protectors does Rose Empire supply?

We manufacture and supply WQMP waterproof quilted, QMP quilted and hotel-grade terry waterproof mattress protectors for commercial hospitality and healthcare buyers.

Are Rose Empire products OEKO-TEX certified and UK tested?

Yes. Our waterproof quilted mattress protectors are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified. We also provide an independent UK test report—download the PDF or visit our certifications section.

How do I request a wholesale quote from Rose Empire?

Add products to the quote cart on this site, complete the RFQ form and download your PDF quote. Or call +44 7999 988450 or email info@roseempire.co.uk for B2B trade pricing.
