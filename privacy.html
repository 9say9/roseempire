Privacy policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Rose Empire Wholesale Home Textiles ("Rose Empire", "we") operates roseempire.co.uk. This policy explains how we collect and use personal data when you request a quote, checkout online, or contact us.

Data we collect

  • Quote / RFQ forms: name, company, email, phone, delivery address, order notes
  • Stripe checkout: payment is processed by Stripe; we receive order confirmation and customer email — we do not store card numbers
  • Contact: email and phone when you call or write to us
  • Analytics: we may use server logs and search-console data in aggregated form

How we use your data

  • Respond to wholesale enquiries and process orders
  • Send quotes, invoices and delivery updates
  • Improve our website and product range
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations

Legal basis (UK GDPR)

We process data on the basis of contract (fulfilling orders), legitimate interests (B2B sales follow-up), and consent where you opt in to marketing.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run our business: Stripe (payments), email hosting, and couriers for delivery. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Order and quote records are kept for up to 7 years for accounting. Marketing contacts can be removed on request.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion or restriction of your data by emailing info@roseempire.co.uk. You may complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for cart and theme preferences stored in your browser (localStorage). Stripe checkout may set cookies on their domain during payment.
